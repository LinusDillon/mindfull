Always With You

All your mindful reminders are stored on your watch, so they work even when you are away from your phone.

Always Current

The simple configuration page make it easy to update your reminders at any time from your phone.

Always Fresh

By default some randomness will be applied to which prompts appear when, so they are harder to tune out. Of course you can add strictly scheduled reminders too.

Always Aware

Reminders respect your do-not-disturb settings. They can also be set to only appear after a certain amount of inactivity, or only during certain times of day.
